## A-Book charts {#abook}

**Hedge Status Rate** — the **overall success rate** of hedge fills. **Slippage
Detection** — hedge fill price versus expected. **Latency Detection** — hedge
round-trip latency over time. **LP volume share** — how hedge volume splits
across LPs.

## B-Book charts {#bbook}

**Trader Concentration** — the **top traders by volume**, showing how much of the
book a few clients represent. **Top Symbols** — most-traded instruments. **Top
Profitable** — the most profitable clients. **Gross** — gross volume.
