## Symbol mapping {#mapping}

The page proposes **Auto-Map Suggestions** matching each **NexDay Symbol** to an
MT5 symbol, with a **Trend** indicator. You can **Bulk Upload** mappings (the
**Expected columns** are shown), review **Upload History**, or **Clear All
Mappings**.
