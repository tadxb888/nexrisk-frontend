## What it is {#what}

The **Trading Economics** feed supplies the economic calendar (scheduled news
events) that drives NEWS-condition price and hedge rules and news alerts. **Feed
enabled** turns it on; the **Feed summary** shows current status and **Service**
health, and **Recent changes** lists recent config edits.
