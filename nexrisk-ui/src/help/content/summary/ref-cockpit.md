## Tiles {#tiles}

The **NexRisk Cockpit** is the at-a-glance firm view. **Who Is My Risk** surfaces
the **Critical traders** currently flagged. **Behavioral classification**
summarises how the trader base is classified, and **Active risk clusters** shows
how many behavioural clusters are live. **Risk Manager Performance** tracks
desk/operator outcomes, and **Hedge coverage** shows how much client exposure is
currently hedged.
